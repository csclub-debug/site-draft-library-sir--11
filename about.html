About the Library

The Sir M V Science College Library is the academic hub of learning resources for students and faculty. It provides access to books, journals, digital resources, and research materials. With modern cataloging and an e-library facility, it supports both curriculum and competitive exam preparation.

Types of Books

  • 📘 Science & Technology
  • 📗 Arts & Humanities
  • 📙 Competitive Exam Preparation
  • 📕 Research Journals & Reference
  • 📓 Magazines & Periodicals

Library Sitting Area

The library offers a spacious, well-lit sitting area with comfortable reading zones. Students can make use of:

  • Quiet study rooms for self-learning
  • Group discussion tables for collaborative study
  • Computer systems with internet for research
  • Relaxed seating with natural light
